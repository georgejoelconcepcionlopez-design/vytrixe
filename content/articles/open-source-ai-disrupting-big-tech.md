The landscape of artificial intelligence is changing. While closed models from tech giants once held a lead, the rise of open-source AI is leveling the playing field.

Developers and researchers worldwide are collaborating on high-performance models that can be run locally, offering privacy and customization that enterprise solutions often lack.

This shift is forcing big tech companies to rethink their strategies, with some even releasing their own models to maintain relevance in the growing open-source ecosystem.
